Nujabes Tribute Page

Biography

Jusn Seba February 7, 1974- February 26, 2010 born in Minato, Tokyo, Japan,was a Japanese producer, DJ, and composer who mixed hip-hop, jazz, and atmospheric sound. He was the owner of record stores T Records and Guinness Records and found of Hydeout Productions. Nujabes collaborated with many other Japanese artists. His most reconizable work is background music from the amime series Samurai Champloo.

Jun Seba passed away in a traffic accident February 26, 2010
